# MergeX Customer Lifecycle System

This is the high-level lifecycle architecture for MergeX

The system is divided into:

1. Lead Operations (Pre-Sales Layer)
2. Sales Conversion (Conversion Layer)
3. Client Execution (Post-Sales Layer)

Each layer has:

* a clear operational purpose,
* defined progression,
* and non-overlapping responsibilities.

---

# PHASE A - LEAD OPERATIONS

# (Pre-Sales Layer)

## Purpose

Identify, understand, qualify, and nurture potential opportunities before investing sales-conversion effort.

This phase is primarily:

* research-driven,
* qualification-driven,
* and opportunity-focused.

Most leads will stay in this layer.

---

# 1. Lead Intake

## Purpose

Capture and organize all incoming opportunities into the system.

---

## Lead Sources

* Inbound
* Outbound
* Referrals
* Partnerships
* Events
* LinkedIn
* Instagram
* WhatsApp
* Website
* Email

---

## Includes

* source tagging
* contact capture
* owner assignment
* lead entry timestamp
* basic business details

---

## Output

A raw lead enters the system.

---

# 2. Business Review

## Purpose

Research and analyze the business before outreach or qualification.

This stage combines:

* lead enrichment,
* business analysis,
* opportunity discovery,
* and pain-point identification.

---

## Includes

### Business Research

* company/business profile
* industry
* size
* location
* social presence
* website/ecommerce presence
* business model
* operational maturity

---

### Opportunity Analysis

* growth gaps
* operational inefficiencies
* missing systems
* conversion bottlenecks
* retention weaknesses
* customer experience issues
* monetization gaps

---

### Strategic Positioning

* outreach angle
* probable pain points
* relevant MergeX services
* value proposition direction

---

## Example

```txt id="hgyfpy"
Menswear brand
130k followers
Strong engagement
No ecommerce system
No CRM
No retention workflow

Opportunity:
Ecommerce + retention automation
```

---

## Output

A clear understanding of:

* the business,
* the probable problems,
* and the opportunity angle.

---

# 3. Lead Qualification

Determine whether the lead is commercially and operationally worth pursuing.

---

## Qualification Areas

* growth potential

## Output

# MergeX Document System Architecture

# (Finalized Operational Document Flow)

This is the finalized document architecture for [MergeX](https://mergex.in?utm_source=chatgpt.com).

The system is structured around:

* customer lifecycle progression,
* operational maturity,
* low-friction sales,
* scalable consulting delivery,
* and progressive onboarding.

---

# CORE DOCUMENT PHILOSOPHY

The document system should follow:

# trust progression.

As client commitment increases:

* document depth increases,
* operational detail increases,
* information collection increases.

The complete document system is divided into:

| Layer                      | Purpose                       |
| -------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Sales Discussion Documents | understanding & communication |
| Commercial Documents       | pricing & conversion          |
| Legal Documents            | commitment & protection       |
| Transition Documents       | sales → execution handoff     |

| Execution Documents        | delivery operations           |
| Closure Documents          | completion & retention        |
